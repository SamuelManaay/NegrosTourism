Discover Our Natural Wonders

Explore the diverse beauty of Negros Oriental

Beach

Manjuyod Sandbar

Dubbed the "Maldives of the Philippines," Manjuyod Sandbar in Bais is a breathtaking stretch of white sand that appears during low tide.

Explore Manjuyod
Waterfall

Pulangbato Falls

Located in Valencia, Pulangbato Falls is famous for its unique reddish rocks and refreshing pools, surrounded by lush greenery.

Explore Pulangbato
Waterfall

Casaroro Falls

A hidden gem in Valencia, Casaroro Falls features a dramatic 100-foot drop and is accessible via a scenic trek through the forest.

Explore Casaroro
Lakes

Twin Lakes

Nestled in Sibulan, the Twin Lakes of Balinsasayao and Danao are a tranquil natural park perfect for kayaking and bird watching.

Explore Twin Lakes
Island

Apo Island

Apo Island is a world-famous marine sanctuary known for its vibrant coral reefs and opportunities to swim with sea turtles.

Explore Apo Island
Landmark

Tierra Alta Lighthouse

This iconic lighthouse in Valencia offers panoramic views of the mountains and sea, and is popular for photos and events.

Explore Tierra Alta

Welcome to Negros Oriental

Greetings, travelers everywhere who yearn for nature at its most natural. Negros Oriental is what you are looking for. Our Province is endowed with such diversity in landscape, waters, and people.

We have any number of falls and caves, beaches and dive sites, mountains, lakes, rugged terrain for adventure, serene sites for leisure. Everyone awaits to welcome you with a warm, Dayon!

When you have to make a choice: Negros Oriental, Naturally!
