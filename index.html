Discover Our Natural Wonders

Explore the diverse beauty of Negros Oriental

Beach

Manjuyod Sandbar

Dubbed the "Maldives of the Philippines," Manjuyod Sandbar in Bais is a breathtaking stretch of white sand that appears during low tide. Enjoy dolphin watching, floating cottages, and island hopping in this unique paradise.

Explore Manjuyod
Waterfall

Pulangbato Falls

Located in Valencia, Pulangbato Falls is famous for its unique reddish rocks and refreshing pools, surrounded by lush greenery. The distinctive rusty-red color comes from the sulfur deposits in the area.

Explore Pulangbato
Waterfall

Casaroro Falls

A hidden gem in Valencia, Casaroro Falls features a dramatic 100-foot drop and is accessible via a scenic trek through the forest. The narrow stream of water creates a mesmerizing sight worth the journey.

Explore Casaroro
Lakes

Twin Lakes

Nestled in Sibulan, the Twin Lakes of Balinsasayao and Danao are a tranquil natural park perfect for kayaking, bird watching, and scenic boat rides. Surrounded by lush rainforest, these crater lakes offer a peaceful escape.

Explore Twin Lakes
Island

Apo Island

Apo Island is a world-famous marine sanctuary known for its vibrant coral reefs, abundant marine life, and opportunities to swim with sea turtles. It's a must-visit for snorkeling, diving, and eco-adventures.

Explore Apo Island
Landmark

Tierra Alta Lighthouse

This iconic lighthouse in Valencia offers panoramic views of the mountains and sea, and is a popular spot for photos and events. Explore the Greek-inspired architecture and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Explore Tierra Alta

Welcome to Negros Oriental

Greetings, travelers everywhere who yearn for nature at its most natural. Negros Oriental is what you are looking for. Our Province is endowed with such diversity in landscape, waters, and people that it is arguably the richest of tourism destinations in the Visayas.

We have any number of falls and caves, beaches and dive sites, mountains, lakes, rugged terrain for adventure, serene sites for leisure. At every turn is a feast for the senses. Everyone awaits to welcome you with a warm, Dayon!

When you have to make a choice: Negros Oriental, Naturally!
