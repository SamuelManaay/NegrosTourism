Accommodation Tips

  • Peak Season: Book accommodations 2-3 months in advance if traveling during December-May, especially around Holy Week.
  • Locations: Stay in Dumaguete City for urban convenience, Dauin for diving, Valencia for mountains, and Siaton or Bais for more secluded experiences.
  • Transportation: Many resorts offer airport transfers - inquire during booking.
  • Local Options: Consider pension houses and homestays for budget-friendly authentic experiences.
